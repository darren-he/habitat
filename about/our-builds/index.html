Our Builds

We’re building! We currently have three ongoing construction projects: Balina Place, Fairmount Street, and Main Street in Malden.

In Progress Builds
Completed Builds

Balina Place

Habitat just finished construction of two homes at 15 and 17 Balina Place, which are complete and occupied. We also acquired the land next door and are now building three more homes at 7, 9, and 11 Balina Place. This 5-home condominium development is located in the Codman Square area of Dorchester in Boston. Dorchester is our Neighborhood Revitalization Focus neighborhood.

By public transportation: Balina Place is half a mile from the Talbot Avenue commuter rail stop and less than a mile from the Shawmut T stop.

Fairmount Street

Habitat is renovating a two family home located at 172 Fairmount Street in Codman Square in Dorchester, our Neighborhood Revitalization Focus neighborhood. We are replacing the roof, wiring, plumbing, replacing the kitchens and bathrooms, and installing new heating systems and windows. When completed, we will convert it to condominium homes for purchase by Habitat families. 

Main Street, Malden

Located on Main Street in Malden, Habitat’s newest development will become eight affordable homes for local low-income families.  Habitat was selected as the developer of the land by the Malden Redevelopment Authority and is part of Habitat’s focus on communities north of Boston.  We’re breaking ground in October!
